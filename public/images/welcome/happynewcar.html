#HappyNewCar

We love seeing pictures of our happy customers

Receiving delivery of a beautiful new car doesn’t happen every day, it’s something to celebrate, and we want to share in your excitement! Send us a selfie with your new car and you could win SMART insurance that’ll cover you in the event of any dents and scratches* to your new motor. Every month we’ll choose our favourite from all the pictures posted to us on Facebook, Twitter or Instagram using the hashtag #HappyNewCar and the winner will receive free SMART insurance for the lifetime of their contract.
*In accordance with SMART's terms and conditions.
