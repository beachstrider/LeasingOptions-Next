Vehicle Leasing Responsibilities

A quick reminder of some of the key things you need to do during the life of your vehicle agreement:

  • Ensure the monthly rentals are paid, in full and on time.

  • Keep the vehicle comprehensively insured at all times.

  • Service and maintain the vehicle in accordance with the manufacturer's recommendations.

  • Ensure that the vehicle is legal and roadworthy at all times.

Fair Wear & Tear

At the end of the agreement you need to return your vehicle in good condition in line with its age, mileage and free from any dents, scratches or interior/exterior damage.

Below you will find a copy of the BVRLA's fair wear & tear guide which helps to explain the expected interior and exterior condition of the vehicle when it is returned at the end of the lease agreement, we request you take the time to read it.
